Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

At CollegeDwar, we collect personal details like your name, phone number, email address, and educational preferences through our website forms and lead generation tools. This information is used strictly to provide you with expert admission guidance.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalized 1-on-1 career counselling.
  • To contact you regarding college updates, MBA programs, and partner university news (e.g., NMIMS, Manipal).
  • To share admission timelines and milestone alerts via WhatsApp or Email.

3. Data Security & Sharing

Your privacy is our priority. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third-party marketing agencies. Data is only shared with partner institutions when necessary for your specific admission application process.

4. Consent

By using this website and submitting your details, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. You can opt-out of communications at any time by contacting our support team.
